Color Calibration

Color calibration is really impossible to do without some standard hard-copy color chart reference. The things are futher complicated by the different color spaces different devices use. It's a big topic, and a lot of commercial companies rake in dollars providing ready to use solutions. I don't have enough experience to tell you how to color-calibrate your system, so you'll just have to figure it out for yourself. On the bright side, look at the test patterns below and enjoy pretty colors :).

Primary colors should appear clean and clear, grays neutral, and transitions between colors in hue charts continuous and really smooth. If there is banding, you are in low-color mode. A lot can be learned about your monitor/video card just by looking at grayscale gradients.
